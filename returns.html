Returns & Exchanges

At Animz Max, your satisfaction is our priority. If you're not happy with your order, we’re here to help.

1. Return Eligibility

You may request a return or exchange within 7 days of receiving your order. Items must be:

  • Unworn and unwashed
  • In their original packaging with tags
  • Free of any stains, odors, or damage

2. How to Initiate a Return

To start a return or exchange, email us at support@animzmax.com with the following details:

  • Your Order ID
  • Item(s) you wish to return/exchange
  • Reason for the return/exchange
  • Photos of the product if it's damaged/defective

3. Return Shipping

You are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item received was defective or incorrect. We recommend using a trackable shipping service.

4. Exchanges

Exchanges are subject to product availability. If the item requested is unavailable, we will notify you and offer alternatives or a refund.

5. Refunds

Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you via email. If approved, your refund will be processed to the original payment method within 5–7 business days.

6. Non-returnable Items

  • Sale/clearance items
  • Gift cards
  • Custom or personalized products
  • Face masks or intimate apparel (for hygiene reasons)

7. Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven’t received a refund after 7 business days, please:

  • Check your bank or credit card statement
  • Contact your bank or credit card company
  • If still unresolved, contact us at support@animzmax.com

8. Policy Updates

We reserve the right to modify this Returns & Exchanges policy at any time. Any changes will be updated on this page.
